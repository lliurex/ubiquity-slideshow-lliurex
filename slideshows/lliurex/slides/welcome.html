Thank you for choosing LliureX 19.07

LliureX is an initiative driven by the Department of Education of Regional Government with the purpose of using free software ICTs in the Valencian Education System, so as to allow its users to enjoy a thoughtful and fine user experience.

LliureX is a Custom Ubuntu GNU/Linux Distribution (CDD) intended as an operating system for educational institutions.

While LliureX is installed, this slideshow will show you around its main features.
