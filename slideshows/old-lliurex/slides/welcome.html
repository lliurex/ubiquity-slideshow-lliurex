LliureX 12.06 Nemo

  • Thank you for choosing LliureX 12.06 Nemo!
  • LliureX is an initiative driven by the Department of Education of Regional Government with the purpose of using free software ICTs in the Valencian Education System.
  • LliureX is a Custom Debian GNU/Linux Distribution (CDD) developed by the Department of Education intended as an operating system for educational institutions throughout the region.
